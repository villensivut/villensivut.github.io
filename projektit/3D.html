My 3D Printer

The finished printer





I had been reading about 3D printers for a while from the Reprap forums and other sites.
It all started when a friend of mine told me I'd never get one built and working.
I decided to prove him wrong and get to it!

So I started to make my shopping list for parts in late August 2012 after deciding on the MendelMax 1.5 frame designed by Kludgineer. The plastics were printed in Hong Kong by tommyc, orange parts in PLA, black parts in ABS. Aluminium extrusions, leadscrews, resin nuts, belts and pulleys from Misumi. Bought horribly noisy chinese LM10UU and LM8UU linear bearings, 30A 12v PSU, 8mm and 10mm precision steel rod and various other stuff from Ebay. Nema 17 stepper motors were also bought from Ebay from Primopal motors. I bought all of the nuts and bolts from local hardware stores. First parts arrived in September, took me a month to self-source all parts down to the last bolt.

Plastics arrived from tommyc, still waiting for extrusions so I can start building the frame!
Y-axis WIP with the heated print bed

So I made a few rookie mistakes while ordering the steppers. (2.3A motors apparently dont like only getting ~1.5A,) After a slight delay finally got the new stepper for extruder at the end of December and I was finally ready to try printing something. Had trouble with faster prints, infill shake caused the stepper to skip steps or the pololu to shut down from overcurrent. I felt that no amount of tuning of my knockoff pololu would help with that. With slower print speed I had no problems, but I had a few extra 1,7A steppers lying around from my last order so I guess it might be worth the trouble to switch. The linear bearings I got from China are really noisy and had some friction, ordered some proper self lubricating bearings from Misumi. Lots of calibration to do.

Third calibration print in progress

I finished my 3D printer just in time for new year's eve in December 2012. At the beginning of January I pretty much got everything dialed in. I hooked up the LCD controller panel which allows for printing directly from an SD card and control of the printer without a computer.

0,1mm layer height calibration print

Maximum print size 23cm x 25cm x 17.5cm(X,Y,Z)
Print material 3mm red ABS, J-Head Mk V-B Hot End-0.35mm, 10mm Y and Z precision smooth rod with crappy chinese LM10UU, 8mm X precision smooth rod also with very noisy chinese LM8UU, Misumi leadscrews and resin nuts for Z, PCB Heatbed MK2a designed by Josef Prusa, 3mm window glass, Arduino mega2560 rev3, Ramps 1.4, pololu 4988 stepper drivers, Nema 17 1.7A 57oz-for extruder, 65oz-in 2.3A for everything else. 30A 12V Power Supply, GT2 belts & 32 tooth pulleys and Misumi 20x20mm aluminum extrusion. Bought plastics from waku5678, orange parts in PLA, black parts in ABS. ~500 pcs nuts&bolts from local hardware stores.
